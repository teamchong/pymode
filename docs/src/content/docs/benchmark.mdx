Three Python serverless workloads, all running on this site's worker
(`pymode.teamchong.net`). Click **Run benchmark** to measure live from
your browser — there are no baked-in numbers.

<Benchmark />

Each workload posts a small body to one of the worker endpoints and
times the full round-trip with `performance.now()`. The reported cold
sample is the first request after the chosen idle pause; warm p50 / p99
come from the remaining iterations sorted by latency. Numbers include
network round-trip from your machine to the closest Cloudflare PoP.
